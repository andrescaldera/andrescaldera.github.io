mallorca's beauty

This set of photos was taken across the island of Mallorca, in December. To me, the natural beauty of the island is vast. Rocky mountains populated with year-round vegetation and often leading up to the shores, where the contrast between mountain and sea spectacular. One fundamental advise to get the most out of the island is to tour it in a vehicle, car rentals are not expensive during winter (neither is anything else) and the island’s fair size allows you to visit many places within the same day while covering enough ground to see changes in the scapes.
