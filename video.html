Fly over the Barceloneta

Feel the first light as it hits Barceloneta beach in Barcelona. This is a short video first intended for instagram, where I publish most of my travel content (this explains the black bars on the sides of the video).

Gardens of Versailles

Experience the stunning gardens of Versailles Palace! Dany dreamed to visit the castle since she was a girl, this video captures an afternoon in the wonders of the gardens surrounding the magnificent french castle. Bright colors, perfect weather and impeccable music - the same kind of music that plays everywhere in the gardens!.

Jordi Morera en #CookingTomorrow | CIB

Cuestión de Futuro | CIB

Video marketing campaign for the Culinary Institute of Barcelona, an upcoming international cooking centre. 7-10 Promotional videos were produced, directed by Carlos Rivero http://ccrivero.com/.

Skating University – Manhattan, Kansas

Manhattan is a beautiful, college-centered town in Kansas. The goal with this project was to indirectly showcase and highlight the campus to potential new students, preserving the academic spirit of the campus while highlighting the extreme (“cool”) vibe it can host.

Annecy Ville, Extreme sports Paradise in France

The city of Annecy is quite the hidden jewel for outdoor activities. They have no shortage of outlets for sports for every-season. Here we wanted to highlight Annecy’s beauty through some of the sports that can be done during Summer.

Down-hill Skate Competition in Paris, France

Promo film of the 3rd edition of Menildescente, a yearly-skate competition organized by the Skate Association RiderZ of Paris. Dozens of participants, thousands of spectators, a unique event that brings downhill skateboarding to the streets of the beautiful city.

Barcelona on Wheels - Spain

A short-video that aims to show how Barcelona’s Urban scene greatly involves transportation on wheels, with countless spots for skateboarding. Giving the city its well deserved reputation of skate-paradise.
