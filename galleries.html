Paris in Blue and Yellow

Early in a Spring morning the center of Paris consistently showed me tones of Blue and Yellow across several different places. These pics were captured between 8-10h, I skated between spots to move faster.

Sunsets in Paris

The Golden hour produces the most intense colors to admire, from the top of the sky down to the ground everything seems to come alive. This is a collection of Sunset photographs I’ve captured all over Paris.

Light at La Défense

La splanade de la Défense is a large area in Paris where modern buildings impose a corporate feel hard to ignore. This is a series of pictures I took during a bright day, admiring how the Architecture played with the variant colors and light.

La Philharmonie de Paris

Shoot for La Philharmonie de Paris, about the design of its façade. The building serves as a space for art and culture development, as well as being a grand concert hall. Building designed by Jean Nouvel.

Fire Skies

Ever-present, variant, passionate, infinite; The sky shows no limits. Most of these are captures of western European skies, during the months of summer/fall.

Details of the Sacre-Coeur

The Basilica is mostly built from white stone and during sunsets I began to notice how its surface reflects the colored light it receives. When placed against the colored sky it makes a quite natural game of colors.

Banner Photos

This is a small collection on photographs I’ve chosen for the Website’s banner. I really like them!.

London

The most incredible city I’ve ever visited. The few days I spent there were not nearly enough to capture its mighty vibe, these pictures slightly hint the variety of sights you find there.
